About Us

"Empowering India's Financial Journey Since 1987"

Our Story

JS Bank (Jana Seva Bank) began as a small community bank in Mumbai with a simple mission: to make banking accessible to everyone. Today, we serve over 5 million customers nationwide, blending decades of trust with modern digital solutions.

Mission

"To simplify banking and drive financial inclusion for every Indian."

Vision

"Be the most trusted partner in our customers' financial growth by 2030."

Core Values

Integrity

Transparent and ethical banking practices.

Innovation

Inclusion

Serving students, rural communities, and small businesses.

Impact

Supporting education and entrepreneurship.

Key Milestones
